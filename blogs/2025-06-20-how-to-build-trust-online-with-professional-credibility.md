Here’s the deal: Every time you pop up online with a different headshot, a tweaked job title, or a bio that sounds like it was written by three different people on three different days, you’re not just being quirky. You’re quietly chipping away at your credibility. Those little inconsistencies create one big problem: Cognitive friction.
